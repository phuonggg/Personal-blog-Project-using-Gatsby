## What Does Google Know About You?

How Is Your Google Data Gathered?

Android: 1.4 billion active users
Chrome: 1 billion monthly-active users
YouTube: 1.8 billion monthly-active users

Find out what Google thinks about you

http://www.google.com/settings/ads/

How Do I Say: Stop Gathering Data About Me, Google!

Extreme way: stop using Google
Android -> iOS
Google Search/Chrome -> Firefox browser + DuckDuckGo and/or WolframAlpha

Ways to limit how much Google knows about you
Google Activity controls
https://myaccount.google.com/activitycontrols

Customize the privacy settings on your Google account
https://myaccount.google.com/?hl=en

opt out from Google sharing your information for Google Analytics
https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
